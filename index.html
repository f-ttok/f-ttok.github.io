Fuminori TATSUOKA (立岡文理)

I'm a Ph.D. course student at the Graduate School of Engineering of Nagoya University.

My research interest is numerical analysis, especially numerical algorithms for matrix functions.

Email: f-tatsuoka[at]na.nuap.nagoya-u.ac.jp

Address: Department of Applied Physics, Graduate School of Engineering Nagoya University, Furo-cho, Chikusa-ku, Nagoya 464-8603, Japan
